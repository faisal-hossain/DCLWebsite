AEC CAD Services

Architectural, Engineering, and Construction drawings can be produced from sketches, paper drawings and raster images.Sketches and accompanied information from clients are read and then drawings are created in CADD format (dwg, dgn or dxf). Produced drawings conform to clients standards for layer/level convention, fonts, text styles, dimension styles etc. All drawings are checked against source drawing carefully by experienced Engineers.Software used for architectural/structural design and drafting include AutoCAD, Microstation, X-Steel etc.

  • Raster to Vector Conversion
  • Steel Detailing
  • Structural Drafting
  • Architectural Drafting
  • Parcel Map
  • Customization and Software Programming

GIS Data Layer

Our services include digitization of geo-spatial features, data extraction of attributes from raster image or paper maps, data entry into database and linking of database to drawing for GIS projects.Maps are digitized manually using CAD overlay method where raster image of the map is used as a backdrop. Both the drawing and the data are checked for correctness before linking them together.Software used for GIS data layer work includes AutoDesk's AutoCAD Map, ESRI's ArcGIS.

  • Base Map Digitization
  • Spatial Feature Creation and Integration
  • Tabular Attributes, Data Entry, Linking and Verification

Architectural Visualization

Our services include creation of photo realistic rendered architectural perspective of 3D exterior and interior from architectural plan, elevation and specification sheet (for paint, color, material type etc.). All 3D models so produced are animation ready.High quality animation work can be completed in relatively short amount of time in low or high detail. Architectural fly-by and walk-through in various formats, e.g., AVI, MPEG-I, MPEG-II etc. can be produced as well.Software used for architectural rendering and animation include AutoCAD, 3D Studio Max, Adobe Photoshop etc.etc.

  • 3D Perspective Rendering
  • Plan and Elevation Rendering
  • Animation

Computer Graphic Design

We can provide our client with eye-catching design for printed media as well for web page using high quality graphics, 3d animation and flash animation.Brochure, Poster, Flyer, Office Stationary are designed to complement clients taste and to reflect the nature of the business.Dynamic web pages are designed with DHTML, flash, java script and java. Simple and fully interactive web page with database connectivity can be designed to meet the need of your organization.Software used are Macromedia Suit, Photoshop, CorelDraw etc.

  • Brochure, Poster, Flyer, Office
  • Stationary for printed media
  • Electronic and Multimedia Content
  • Webpage
